Stony Brook Astronomy

Astronomy @ Stony Brook

The Astronomy Group is part of the Department of Physics and Astronomy. We offer an undergraduate Astronomy major and minor, and graduate students work toward their PhD in Physics in our group. Our research focus spans observational stellar and extra-galactic astronomy, high-energy and nuclear astrophysics, algorithm development, and observational and theoretical cosmology.

Major or Minor in Astronomy

Astronomy majors and minors learn problem-solving skills and how to apply physics to understanding the Universe around them. Many of our Astronomy Undergraduates do research in the group during the semester or over the summer. Our graduates often go on to graduate school and find jobs at National Laboratories, observatories, industry, and academia.

Events

We have several weekly events to discuss research trends. Students are always welcomed to join us.

  • Astronomy Seminars

    We have weekly Astronomy Seminars, generally on Wed. at 1:30p in our seminar room, ESS 450.

  • Joint Stony Brook / BNL Cosmology Seminars

    Alternating biweekly with the Astronomy seminars are the Joint Stony Brook / BNL Cosmology. They are held either during our weekly seminar time or at BNL on Thurs. at 3pm in building 510.

  • Departmental Colloquia

    The Physics and Astronomy colloquia are on Tues. at 4:15p in Harriman 137. Cookies and coffee begin at 4pm.

  • Weekly Coffee

    Our group has a weekly, social coffee on Thurs. 10:30a in our coffee room, ESS 437. Anyone is welcome to join.

  • Astro-ph discussion

    The Astronomy group meets weekly on Wed. at 10:30a in ESS 437 to discuss recent papers on astro-ph. Use the Stony Brook Vox Charta page to suggest papers.

  • Astronomy Open Nights

    The Astronomy Open Night is a public lecture series that takes place generally on the first Friday of each month during the semester (starting at 7:30pm in ESS 001). If the weather is good, viewing through our roof-top telescope follows the lecture.

  • Undergraduate Astronomy Club

    The Stony Brook Astronomy Club meets weekly during the semester. Undergraduates in the club use our rooftop telescope and organize many events throughout the year.
