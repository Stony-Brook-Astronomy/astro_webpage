Detection and Applications of CMB Lensing
Speaker: Alexander van Engelen (McGill)

The lensing of the cosmic microwave background by large-scale structure has been robustly detected by two experimental groups during the previous year.  I will discuss the use of this effect to measure cosmic growth and structure, as well as to help understand properties of galaxies at high redshift.  I will also address the prospects for further improvements in precision, as well as data analysis challenges, with upcoming datasets.
