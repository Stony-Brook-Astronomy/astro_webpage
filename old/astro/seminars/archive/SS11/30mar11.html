Title : Observing at Mt. Stony Brook and Other Activities of The Astronomy Club Speakers : Stony Brook Astronomy Club

Matthew Wahl, Jonathan Ullman, Shannon Hicks, and Adam Siegel will be talking about what observational projects are being conducted at Mt. Stony Brook; as well as what other activities The Astronomy Club is involved in. The presentation will include image analysis, color filters, using a camera to observe with a telescope, and the basic fundamentals of a CCD camera.
