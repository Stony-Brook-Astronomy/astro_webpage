Title : "Inferring the Physical Properties of Brown Dwarfs from Near-Infrared Spectra"
Speaker : Emily Rice, American Museum of Natural History

Accurate measurements of the fundamental physical properties of very-low-mass stars and brown dwarfs are crucial for understanding cool, complex atmospheres and calibrating evolutionary models. We developed methods for evaluating the physical properties of very-low-mass objects as inferred from high-resolution near-infrared spectra. We carried out detailed comparison of observed and synthetic spectra for a sample of young and/or field M, L, and T dwarfs.
