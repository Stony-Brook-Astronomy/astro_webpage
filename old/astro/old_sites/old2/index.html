Stony Brook Astronomy

State University of New York at Stony Brook


Dept. of Physics and Astronomy

 

 

The Stony Brook astronomy group is part of the Department of Physics and Astronomy at SUNY Stony Brook. We are comprised of 10 faculty along with several postdocs and staff scientists, and numerous graduate students. Research opportunities for graduate students include observational astronomy, star formation, extragalactic astronomy and cosmology, and computational and nuclear astrophysics. We are located on the 4th floor of the ESS building.
