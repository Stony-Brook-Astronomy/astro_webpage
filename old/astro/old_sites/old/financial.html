Financial Support

Financial support for undergraduates is primarily provided through the university and its various resources. Some faculty hire undergraduates to help with their research projects, particularly during the summer months and mostly during the latter two years of the undergraduate program.

In the graduate program the department strives to support fully all graduate students in good academic standing. A wide range of research grant support (research assistantships) and university support (fellowships and teaching assistantships) is available. Students also receive support from national fellowships such as those from NSF and NASA and from Stony Brook Graduate Fellowships. In the past, the Program has generally supported all eligible students. Salaries and stipends are competetive with those at other U.S. universities.
