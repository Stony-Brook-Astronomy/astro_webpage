Transportation
Getting to Stony Brook

By Car

From New York City: Take the Long Island Expressway (Route 495) east from the Queens Midtown Tunnel. Take Exit 62 and follow Nichols Road north for nine miles. Turn left at the University main entrance.

By Air

Stony Brook is ten miles north of Long Island MacArthur Airport and 50 miles east of Kennedy International and LaGuardia airports.

By Rail

Take the Long Island Rail Road's Port Jefferson line from Penn Station (Manhattan), Flatbush Avenue Station (Brooklyn), or Jamaica Station. Change trains at Jamaica or Huntington per LIRR timetable. Train stops at Stony Brook. Campus bus service is available at a nominal charge.

By Ferry

Year-round service is provided by the Bridgeport and Port Jefferson Ferry Co. and by Cross Sound Ferry Services, Inc., the latter providing crossings between New London, Connecticut and Orient Point at the eastern tip of Long Island.
