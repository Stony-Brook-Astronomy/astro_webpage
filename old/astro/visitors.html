Visiting the Astronomy Group at Stony Brook

Location:

Earth & Space Sciences Bldg.
Stony Brook University, Main Campus
(We are on the 4th floor, marked PH for PentHouse in the elevator).

 

Air Travel Recommendations

The closest airport is Long Island MacArthur Airport (airport designator ISP) at Islip NY which is about 16 miles (25 minutes) from Stony Brook. This is a regional airport served mainly by Southwest. In recent years, flight options here have become more limited.

It is also possible to fly into LaGuardia (LGA) or John F Kennedy (JFK) airports, with travel times ranging from 1 hour (no traffic) to 1.5–2 hours (regular rush hour traffic). Newark NJ (EWR) is also an option, but it is much further away and can take 2.5 hours because of heavy metropolitan area traffic.

More info about NY area metropolitan airports is available at the Port Authority Web Site

 

Rail, Ferry, and Car Travel Directions

Directions and information about how to get to Stony Brook by Rail, Ferry, and Car are available at the Stony Brook Directions Web Site

The Astronomy group is on the top floor of the Earth & Space Science Building. You can find this building in grid C-4 on the online campus map. Proceed to the 4th floor (marked PH for PentHouse) on the elevator buttons.

  • Holiday Inn Express, Stony Brook
