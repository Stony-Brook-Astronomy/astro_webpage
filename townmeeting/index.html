Astronomy Town Meeting

for Astronomy majors and minors

The Stony Brook Astronomy faculty would like to hold a "town meeting" with all our majors, minors, and anyone who might be interested in Astronomy, to discuss the undergraduate astronomy program and resources available to students.

Among the topics we would like to discuss are:

  • the current Astronomy course offerings

  • research opportunities for undergraduates

  • access to telescopes / the Undergraduate Astronomy Club

  • applying to graduate school

We'd like to hear your thoughts. Feel free to invite anyone with an interest in the Astronomy major or minor.

Wednesday November 1, 2017
1:00 - 2:30 pm
ESS 450

Pizza will be provided

Please RSVP to michael.zingale AT stonybrook.edu so we can get a head count.
